The studio

Made by hand, one loop at a time

Every piece at Cozy Loop is crocheted from scratch by Joseph Paul. No machines, no mass production, just yarn, patience, and a lot of heart.

From mini swiss rolls that look good enough to eat to squishy little animal keychains, each item is a small handmade keepsake. Perfect as a gift, a bag charm, or a quiet treat for yourself.
