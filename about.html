THE Maker

JOSEPH PAUL

Cozy Loop is a small independent crochet studio run by designer Joseph Paul in Segamat, Johor, Malaysia. Started out of a love for tactile fiber crafts, every item is looped and bound by hand.

In a world saturated with generic mass-produced items and algorithm-generated designs, we believe in the physical value of a handmade keepsake. When you purchase from Cozy Loop, you receive a piece that holds hours of focused attention, care, and quality materials.

MATERIALS GUIDE

YARN SPECIFICATIONS

We use high-grade materials to ensure durability, softness, and color vibrancy.

PROPERTY SPECIFICATION DETAILS
MATERIAL 100% Milk Cotton Yarn (composed of 80% cotton and 20% milk fiber). Offers a silky touch and baby-safe hypo-allergenic properties.
PADDING FILL Premium A-grade virgin Polyester Fiberfill. Maintains springiness, shape retention, and allows easy cleaning.
COLOR DYES Reactive eco-friendly dyes with high colorfastness. Non-fading under normal daylight exposure.
WASH CARE Handwash only in cold water with mild detergent. Air dry flat. Do not wring, tumble dry, iron, or bleach.
STITCH COUNT High-density single crochet gauge (approx. 22 stitches per 10cm row) preventing stuffing leakage.
