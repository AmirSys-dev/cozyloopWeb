HOW IT WORKS

Every commission request is processed manually by Joseph. You can submit details here to add them to your shopping cart, and check out like any regular item.

1. DESIGN STAGES

Describe your creation, specify dimensions, shapes, or send reference images via Instagram after submitting the request.

2. CUSTOM COLORING

Specify colors down to detail parts (e.g. sage base, white wings, pink blush cheeks). We use milk cotton yarn colors matching your requirements.

3. TIMELINE & ESTIMATES

Bespoke crochet takes time. Depending on complexity, crafting takes 3 to 7 working days. Shipping is calculated upon check out.
